Destaque

It came about when the carreteiros (cargo carriers) who crossed Rio Grande do Sul in carts pulled by oxen, cooked a mixture of chopped jerky (stew) and rice in an iron pot. It was a practical dish that, due to its simplicity, could be prepared by the lone traveler, who could only eat a barbecue (made from fresh meat) in a paradouro after many days on the road.
